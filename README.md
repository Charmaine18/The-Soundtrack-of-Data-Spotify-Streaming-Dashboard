A comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed for in-depth analysis of Spotify streaming and listener data. This interactive tool provides a clear, visual overview of music consumption patterns, artist and track performance, and audience demographics, enabling data-driven decision-making for music professionals and enthusiasts.

It serves as a powerful tool for monitoring music performance, understanding global listening trends, and deriving strategic insights for content promotion and creation.

 üìä Key Features & Metrics

The dashboard is structured into multiple pages, each focusing on a distinct area of analysis:

 1. Performance Overview
Total Streams and Total Unique Listeners with Month-over-Month (MoM) and Year-over-Year (YoY) comparisons.
Top 10 Tracks and Top 5 Artists ranked by total stream count.
Stream Velocitytrend line to monitor daily or weekly growth.

 2. Audience Insights
Geographic Map visualization showing streams by country and region.
 Breakdown of streams by Device Type (Mobile, Desktop, Web Player).
 Analysis of Listening Habits by Hour of Day to determine peak engagement times.

3. Content Analysis
Genre Distribution charts (e.g., Treemap or Pie Chart).
Average Audio Features (Danceability, Energy, Valence) for the most popular tracks.
Comparison of performance metrics for tracks from Albums vs. Singles.
